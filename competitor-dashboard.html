🎯 Where Readabull Wins vs BookBub
Gap 1 — No Unified Dashboard
Authors running BookBub Ads AND Amazon Ads have no single place to manage, compare and optimise both. Readabull fills this directly.
Gap 2 — Weak Analytics
BookBub gives basic click data only. No cross-platform attribution, no ROI calculator, no series read-through tracking.
Gap 3 — Author Loses the Customer
BookBub exit-routes to Amazon, which captures the reader relationship. Readabull helps authors build direct reader relationships alongside ad spend.
Gap 4 — High Barrier to Entry
Featured Deals require editorial approval + high flat fees. Authors need guidance on whether to spend there vs BookBub Ads vs AMS — Readabull can advise intelligently.
Gap 5 — No Series Strategy Tooling
Book 1 free → read-through strategy is well known but hard to model. A tool that automates this planning would be highly differentiated.
